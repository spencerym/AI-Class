Using AI to Enhance This Assignment

Exploring how generative AI, like ChatGPT and Deepseek, can improve the creation and delivery of our proposal while embracing systems-based learning.

Background on the Proposal

This project asks us to propose a legitimate use of AI for a past assignment, convincing teachers it enhances learning without subverting goals, all while understanding and leveraging larger systems.

Teacher's Goals for the Assignment

  • Develop critical thinking and problem-solving skills.
  • Practice effective communication and presentation skills.
  • Demonstrate ethical and reflective use of technology.
  • Enhance presentation skills by clearly presenting a proposal
  • Understand systems-based learning and how to navigate systems effectively.

How AI Can Help Us on This Project

  • Help outline the project, telling us what steps to take first.
  • We can still refine the proposal, ensuring that it aligns with our own thoughts
  • Fix grammar issues, enhance clarity, and help organize of arguments.
  • Refining presentation language for professionalism.
  • Understanding the role of AI in larger educational and technological systems.
  • Improve the visuals of the slideshow

Addressing Teacher Concerns

  • Over-reliance on AI: Students still engage in critical thinking.
  • Loss of originality: AI is used for support, not substitution.
  • Ethical use: Clearly disclose where and how AI was used and reflect on its impact.
  • Systems awareness: Highlight how AI fits into broader educational goals and systems.

Enhanced Learning Outcomes

  • Develops digital literacy by critically assessing AI suggestions.
  • Saves time on initial drafts, allowing deeper focus on refining ideas.
  • Encourages reflective practices about the role of technology in education.
  • Builds skills for navigating and utilizing systems to achieve goals effectively.
  • AI can generate practice questions, helping us prepare for Q&A

Conclusion

Using AI for this proposal aligns with the teacher's goals by fostering thoughtful engagement, promoting ethical AI use, and improving the quality of the final product. It also deepens our understanding of systems-based learning and how to leverage systems to achieve objectives.
